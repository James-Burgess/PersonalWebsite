When am I available?

I'm currently not actively looking for any oppertunies at the moment, but if you have something cool you would like my help with, it only takes a calendar month to get me on your payroll.

What is my ideal working week?

I'm a huge fan of working in an asynchronous team with flexible time, focusing on work done not time spent, as writing code is inherently creative and can be mentally taxing. Team communication is always important, Stand-ups are always useful to me and they usually follow some uninterpreted time to crush some tickets. Planning sessions and review of work are the only other meetings that should occur regularly.

What are my salary expectations?

I will not disclose any figures until an offer is made, or a figure is put forwards by an employer that is specific to me. For example: (x >= $salary >= z) is not concrete enough.[1]

1. Recruiters are biased towards pushing whomever quotes the largest salary within some bounds of reason, for commission's sake. Employers are in an asymmetric position where they have complete information about the salary expectations of all applicants and current employees. The most sensible solution to this asymmetry for me is to be given a figure around which salary can be discussed, and that is what I will do. If you do not think this is fair or reasonable, I would ask you to imagine the inverse; you only find out how much you're going to be paying an employee once all others have been eliminated from the pool and are unrecoverable as prospects. Sounds like one of the worst hiring strategies ever! I'm a stickler for fair treatment, and so I ask to be treated fairly in salary negotiations.
